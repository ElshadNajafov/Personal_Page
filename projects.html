Projects

During this semester, I (with my team) worked on two projects. These were Logic Gates (Project 1) and Hour of Code (Project 2).

Simple Logic Gates

On this project, we visited a lab of the university and worked on simple Logic Gates (NOT, AND, OR, NAND, and XOR) on Breadboard. We learnt how they work and why they are necessary.

Hour of Code

In Hour of Code project, we visited a school and made a session with 5th grade students. They learnt basic understandings of coding as we showed what they can do with Scrath programming language.
